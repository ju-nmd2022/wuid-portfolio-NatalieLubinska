Åsa distillery

I have created a brand identity for Åsa distillery. The brand identity include brand colors, typography, logos and a mood board. The folder contains information about the company, the vision for the brand and a style guide for website and packaging. The moodboard visualize the feeling that the company wants to portray. It includes images of the northen woods, campfires and swedish cabbins.
