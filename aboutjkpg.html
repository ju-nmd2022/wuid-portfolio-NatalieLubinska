What you can do in Jönköping

The student life in Jönköping is great. The univerity is located right in the center close to all the restaurants, clubs and other activities. Often you have walking distance home or to your friends from uni. Comparing to my hometown Stockholm, studentlife is alot richer because of the walking distance. Jönköping University has alot of associations that offer different activities. You can easily find an association that suits you, like the sports association, spring association or one of the sexmasterys.

Nightlife and restaurants

The nightlife for students consists mostly of Akademin, the studentclub open on wednesdays. Shooters is a popular sportsbar for students where you can play biliard or shuffleboard. On special occations, like the infamous "tentafest", students arrange events at regular clubs like Glädje. In the summer the restaurant and club Twin City is popular because of the cozy outdoor seating. Honestly I do prefer the restaurants in Stockholm. Here in Jönköping there is a huge amount of pizzerias and kebab, which can be nice sometimes. The restauant Bastard Burgers serve the best hamburgers in my opinion. On mondays the bar Shooters serve student tacos which is a nice tradition.
